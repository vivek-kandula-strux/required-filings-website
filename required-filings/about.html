COMMON QUESTIONS

Quick answers before you call.

Talk to a filing expert
  • Who actually files my returns — a CA or an executive?
    A qualified chartered accountant or company secretary prepares and reviews every filing. You approve the draft before we submit. No filing goes out without expert sign-off.
  • How fast do you respond on WhatsApp or email?
    A real person replies within one working day. During business hours, most messages get answered the same day.
  • Can you take over compliance from my old CA?
    Yes. We collect your past filings, clear pending returns, and take over the recurring schedule. Most takeovers are complete within 30 days.
  • Do you work with businesses in every Indian state?
    Yes. Filings are made online to central and state portals. Your physical location does not affect our service.
  • What happens if a filing gets a notice from the department?
    We reply to the notice on your behalf. For filings we prepared, response and representation are included. No extra fee.
