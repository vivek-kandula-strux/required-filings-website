Your books, closed every month. Tax-ready, audit-ready, lender-ready.

Bookkeeping is the daily record of every rupee in and out of your business. Without clean books, you cannot file taxes accurately, and notices follow. You also cannot raise debt or equity, because lenders ask for last 3 years of P&L and balance sheet.

We keep your books on Tally or Zoho Books, close them every month, and hand over your P&L, balance sheet and cash flow. Your CA gets clean inputs at year end. Your GST returns reconcile every month.

  • Day-to-day bookkeeping
  • Bank and ledger reconciliation
  • Accounts payable and receivable
  • Month-end and year-end closing
  • MIS and management reporting
  • Inventory accounting
  • Payroll accounting
  • Software selection and rollout

How we work with you each month

You share invoices, bills and bank statements. We post entries, reconcile your bank and ledgers, and close the month. You get your reports by the 10th of the next month.

01.
Daily bookkeeping

Sales, purchases, expenses and bank entries posted on Tally or Zoho Books. Structured chart of accounts.

02.
Software setup

We pick, set up and train your team on Tally, Zoho Books, QuickBooks or Xero. Migration included.

03.
Monthly close

P&L, balance sheet and cash flow by the 10th. GST-ready ledgers and TDS workings each month.

  • Do you work on Tally or Zoho Books?
    Both. Tally is the default for trading and manufacturing. Zoho Books works well for service businesses and startups. We also handle QuickBooks and Xero.
  • Will you also file my GST and TDS?
    Yes. Our books feed straight into GST and TDS returns. You get one team for accounting and filings. No vendor hand-offs and no reconciliation gaps.
  • Can you fix two years of pending books?
    Yes. We do back-book write-ups for any period. We rebuild ledgers, reconcile banks and produce P&L and balance sheets for past years.
  • Do you handle payroll too?
    Yes. We run monthly payroll, generate payslips, calculate PF, ESI and TDS on salary, and file Form 24Q. Available from 5 employees and up.
  • What do you need from me each month?
    Sales invoices, purchase bills, expense bills, bank statements and payroll inputs. You drop them in a shared folder. We do the rest.
