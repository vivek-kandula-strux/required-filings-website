MSME stands for Micro, Small and Medium Enterprise. You qualify if your investment in plant and machinery stays under ₹50 crore and your turnover stays under ₹250 crore. Udyam is the free MSME registration on the government portal. You get one Udyam number per business.

Without an Udyam number, you miss the 50% subsidy on patent and trademark fees. You also lose priority access to MSME bank loans and government tender preference. We register you for free and link every benefit you qualify for.

ZED stands for Zero Defect Zero Effect. It is a manufacturing quality certification from the Ministry of MSME. It comes in three levels — Bronze, Silver and Gold. ZED-certified manufacturers get extra subsidies on patents, ISO and testing fees.

Is Udyam registration free?

Yes. The Udyam portal charges nothing. Our fee covers the application, classification check and benefit linkage. You get your Udyam certificate in 2 working days.

Who can apply for ZED certification?

Any registered MSME manufacturer. You must hold a valid Udyam number first. Service businesses and traders are not eligible for ZED.

What benefits do I get with ZED Bronze?

Bronze is the entry level. You get 80% subsidy on certification fees, plus reimbursement on patent, ISO and testing costs. Silver and Gold unlock higher subsidies and tender priority.

Do I need a GST number to register on Udyam?

Only if you are already required to register for GST. For businesses below the GST threshold, your PAN and Aadhaar are enough.

How long does ZED Bronze take?

Most factories reach ZED Bronze in 60 to 90 days. Silver and Gold each add another 60 to 120 days. We handle the audits, evidence and follow-up.
